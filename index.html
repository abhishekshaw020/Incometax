About Income Tax

Paying income tax is essential for nation-building, funding public services, and maintaining infrastructure.

Benefits of Paying Taxes on Time

  • Avoid penalties and legal issues
  • Better credit score
  • Eligible for tax rebates and deductions
  • Contributes to national development

Important Income Tax Sections

  • Section 80C: Deductions on investments (PPF, EPF, LIC, etc.)
  • Section 80D: Deductions on health insurance premiums
  • Section 24(b): Home loan interest deductions
  • Section 87A: Tax rebate for income up to ₹7 lakh
